I saw him again today.

He was standing near the corner of the room, holding that same bag.

It’s too small.

Anyone can see that.

But he keeps trying to push it in — slowly, carefully — like if he forces it enough, it will eventually accept what doesn’t belong.

The zipper barely moves now.

It strains… like it’s holding more than it should.

And for a moment, I thought I saw something spill past the edges —
not outside… but still inside.

That’s the part I don’t understand.

How can something be contained…
and yet not fit at all?

I think the answer isn’t in what we see.

It’s in what the container refuses to show.
