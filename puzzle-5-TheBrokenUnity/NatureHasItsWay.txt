In a lush sanctuary filled with vibrant flora and colorful wings,
hundreds of birds from different lands fly freely together.

Though they come from distant places,
they share the same sky.

This place celebrates harmony in nature,
where diversity lives side by side.

But this harmony reflects something much larger nearby —
a monument that symbolizes many becoming one.

Find the bird that represents this idea.
