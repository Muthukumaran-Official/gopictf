They said it was just background noise.

Something to fill the silence.

I believed them at first.

But when I played it again…
it felt wrong.

Not louder. Not clearer.

Just… out of place.

Like it didn’t belong in the direction it was moving.

I tried to ignore it.

But the more I listened,
the more it felt like something was trying to speak —
just not in the way we’re used to hearing.

Maybe…

it was never meant to be understood forward.
